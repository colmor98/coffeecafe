Membership

Why should you become a member of our Cafè ?

We try to encourage as many people as possible to become members of our cafè as you could become part of one of
Dublin's newest and most-popular cafè resorts in the Ballsbridge area. Get to know some great people and enjoy
some of our freshly brewed coffee straight from our fairtrade coffee farms in Cabana, Brazil.

Our members are eligible to several discounts in all of our branches. Also if you become one of our premium members,
you will become part of our "Free coffee's for our Favourite customers" scheme, where we give our premium members
a free coffee every month just to show our love!
