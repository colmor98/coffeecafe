About Us




We are a small coffee shop located in Ballsbridge, Co. Dublin.
We are gradually expanding and have launched our new website.
Our Coffee Cafè family is expanding at an excellent rate and
is currently recruting. We see all of our workers as family members
because our Cafè originally began as a small family
business. With currently 6 full-time baristas, one head waiter,
one hostess, and 3 waiters, we are looking for new staff to work
in our new cafè in Swords. You can find contact details for
our cafè in the 'Contact Us' section of our website directly below, or else
call into our cafè and ask for Colm, our manager.
