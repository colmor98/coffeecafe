Cafè Menu & Tea List

Coffee

  • Regular Brew

    Traditional drip brewed coffee traceable back to the farm through the batch number of the roast.

  • Cold Brew

    Cold water extracted for 8-16 hours creating an iced coffee with a full-bodied, aromatic flavor profile that translates into a bold, super-smooth taste with reduced acidity.

  • Americano

    Prepared with espresso and hot water, creating a similar strength to drip brew but with the flavor profile of espresso.

  • Red Eye

    Drip brewed coffee with a single shot of espresso.

  • French Press

    Simple immersion brewing that creates a bold full-bodied flavor, exposing all the traits of a coffee. (Serves 2 - 5)

  • Chemex

    Pour-over brewing that creates a clean and crisp brew with little bitterness. (Serves 2 - 4)

  • Clever

    Mix of pour-over and immersion brewing methods capturing the full-bodied flavor of immersions and the clean crisp taste of pour overs. (Serves 1 - 2)

  • Cha Cha Chai

Espresso Bar

  • Espresso

    Water forced through finely ground coffee at high pressure, extracting many coffee notes otherwise undetected.

  • Cafè Latte

    Espresso painted with velvety foamed textured milk.

  • Vanilla/Caramel Latte

    Your classic latte with hints of vanilla or caramel syrup. Ask your barista about other available syrups.

  • Cappuccino

    Espresso with foamed milk. Similar to a latte but with less milk and more foam. Can be made normal, wet or dry.

  • Cafè Mocha

    Espresso, textured milk and rich chocolate. Can be made with white chocolate or mixed(marbled).

  • Espresso Machiato

    Double shot espresso stained with a touch of textured milk.

  • Latte Machiato

    Textured milk stained with a shot of espresso

  • Cortado

    Double shot espresso cut with textured milk. Think latte but with much less milk and prominent espresso.

  • Chai Tea Latte

    Textured milk with spiced chai tea.

  • Hot Chocolate

    Rich chocolate prepared with silky foamed textured milk.

  • Steamer

    Silky textured foamed milk. Perfect for the kids. Add optional Syrup for an additional sweet touch.

Other Teas

  • Pouchong, Oolong, White Tea, Yerba Mate and More
    Iron Goddess of Mercy

    Curled leaf tea from Fujian. Tender peachy notes intermingle with nutty overtones. Oolong tea, medium oxidation.

  • Jasmine Yin Hao

    A traditional jasmine scented pouchong, slightly less oxidized than oolong. Melodic coupling of fragrant petals & light, sweet tea produce a palate for romance. Organic Certified.

  • Pai Mu Tan

    This white tea is smooth & flowery with a hint of earthiness. Organic Certified.

  • Yerba Mate

    South American herb, intense, grassy & highly stimulating. Contains matteine, similar to caffeine. Organic Certified.

  • Coffee Cherry

    The dried cherry and husk from the fruit of the coffee tree. Sweet and pleasant. Trace amounts of caffeine.
